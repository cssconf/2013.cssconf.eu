Information

CSSconf.eu is a one day, one track conference dedicated to the developers and designers who love and write CSS.
With the goal to continue what CSSconf US started earlier this year in Florida, CSSconf.eu will once again unite top notch engineers, world-class speakers, great webdesigners – the people who care about CSS, the CSS community. Be a part of it, join us in Berlin!

Date

CSSconf.eu will happen on September 13.

Venue

CSSconf.eu will be a unique event that focuses on intimacy instead of volume. The historical building of Villa Elisabeth creates the perfect setting. Located in the heart of Berlin and very close to hotels, nightlife, restaurants and the buzzing Mitte district, Villa Elisabeth is a quiet and relaxed gem hidden in a small park. On the day of our event, drones will be flying in the adjacent church as part of the nodecopter hackathon.
