We’re very excited to announce a CSSconf.eu partnership with Smashing Magazine! For many of you Smashing is the daily source of both inspiration and knowledge. This Freiburg-based powerhouse has played a huge role in shaping the design and development community as we know it, not only by supporting various events but also providing quality content for over 7 years already.

Vitaly, Editor-in-Chief (who himself spoke at 35 events in 15 countries) will be joining us as an attendee right after running his own conference, SmashingConf. While the conference itself is already sold out, you can still grab tickets for workshops with Dan Rubin, Dan Mall, Luke Wroblewski or Brad Frost. Make sure to follow @smashingmag’s Twitter feed for updates!
