Information

Being one of the core events in an entire week full of community-driven conferences, hackathons, and social events, CSSconf.eu will gather an international audience of top notch frontend developers and designers in Berlin, Europe’s exciting and vibrant tech hub.

Location

The historical building and former church parish is now an event space for Berlin’s creative scene and offers a unique atmosphere. On the day of our event, the adjacent church and small park will be used for fringe events, like the nodecopter hackathon.

CSSconf.eu will be an amazing event that focuses on intimacy instead of volume.
